National Student’s Space Challenge

National Students’ Space Challenge (NSSC) is India’s first and largest space technology fest conducted by Space Technology Student’s Society at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur. The intent of the fest is to inculcate a culture of space technology, inspire research in the field of space technology, and provide a platform to explore interests in the field of space technology.
- Co-founded India’s first space technology festival for the student community with a participation of 600+ students every year.
- Designed and conducted events for the fest as Technical Head.

Creating Impact in Rural India

India2022 is a business-led coalition committed to leverage the power of collaboration to unlock future-fit growth opportunities in India. Many corporates (Aditya Birla Group, Shell, GE, Cyient, Mahindra etc.) are partners in the coalition working in the areas of energy, waste and sanitation,sustainable mining and healthcare diagnostics.

Partners under India2022 are collectively working to understand and bring together key elements required to accelerate rural growth. Efforts are expected to lead to scalable commercial opportunities that demonstrate social and environmental return. The objective of an integrated approach is to address and overcome some of the systemic challenges to this growth in rural India.
